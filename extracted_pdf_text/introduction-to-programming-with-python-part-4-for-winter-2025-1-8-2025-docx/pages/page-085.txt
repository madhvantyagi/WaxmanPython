The copy module.
Enter the copy module. It contains copy (a shallow copy) and deepcopy ( a deep copy). deepcopy is
much more sophisticated than the recursive copy above. It handles all the data types and edge cases.
The difference between them lies in how they treat complex objects that contain other objects, like lists or
dictionaries.
Shallow Copy (`copy`)
A shallow copy creates a new object, but instead of copying the nested objects, it just copies the
